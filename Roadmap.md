# Roadmap

## Vision

AI Content Engine is on a mission to make social media strategy accessible to every small business. Here's our planned roadmap for the coming months.

## Current Release (v0.1.0)

### ✅ Completed Features

- AI-powered content strategy generation via OpenRouter
- Multi-platform optimization (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn)
- 5-day action plans with specific tasks
- Free tier (5 generations/day) with browser-local tracking
- Pro subscription via Stripe
- Firebase Google Authentication
- Video recommendations for execution

## Upcoming Features

### Phase 1: Enhanced Content Generation (Q2 2026)

- [ ] **Custom Branding Voice**: Save and remember user brand voice preferences
- [ ] **Content Calendar Export**: Export strategies as iCal format
- [ ] **Platform-Specific Scheduling**: Direct scheduling to social platforms
- [ ] **AI Model Selection**: Allow users to choose between different AI models
- [ ] **Content Templates**: Pre-built templates for common industries

### Phase 2: Team & Collaboration (Q3 2026)

- [ ] **Team Workspaces**: Share strategies with team members
- [ ] **Role-Based Access**: Admin, Editor, Viewer roles
- [ ] **Collaborative Editing**: Real-time collaboration on strategies
- [ ] **Team Analytics**: Track team performance and metrics
- [ ] **Email Sharing**: Share strategies via email

### Phase 3: Analytics & Intelligence (Q4 2026)

- [ ] **Performance Tracking**: Monitor posted content performance
- [ ] **Analytics Dashboard**: View metrics from social platforms
- [ ] **AI Recommendations**: Get smarter suggestions based on performance
- [ ] **Competitor Analysis**: Analyze competitor content strategies
- [ ] **Trend Detection**: Identify trending topics in your industry

### Phase 4: Integrations (Q1 2027)

- [ ] **Zapier Integration**: Connect to 1000+ apps
- [ ] **Social Platform APIs**: Direct posting to platforms
- [ ] **CRM Integration**: Connect to HubSpot, Salesforce
- [ ] **Email Marketing**: Connect to Mailchimp, ConvertKit
- [ ] **Analytics Platforms**: Google Analytics, Mixpanel integration
