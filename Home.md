## 🎯 What is AI Content Engine?

ACE helps small businesses generate channel-specific social media strategies in seconds using AI. It provides:

- ✅ **AI-powered strategy generation** via OpenRouter
- ✅ **5-day action plans** tailored to your business
- ✅ **Multi-platform optimization** (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn)
- ✅ **Free tier** (5 generations/day) + **Pro subscription** (unlimited)
- ✅ **Video recommendations** to execute strategies
- ✅ **Firebase Authentication** for user management

## 💡 Key Features

### Content Generation
Generate comprehensive social media strategies including platform-specific plans, hashtag recommendations, and 5-day execution timelines.

### Tiered Pricing
- **Free**: 5 generations per day (browser-local tracking)
- **Pro**: Unlimited generations with Stripe subscription

### Multi-Channel Strategy
Optimized content plans for Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and LinkedIn with platform-specific best practices.
